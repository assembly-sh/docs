Assembly.sh offers developers tools and services that complement cloud computing, enabling their applications to interact at the network layer (edge). This allows the offloading of resource-intensive tasks, traditionally handled by devices, to the edge, including analytics, A/B testing, web performance optimization, content stitching, and more.
